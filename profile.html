About Me

Hey there! I'm Steve, a crochet enthusiast who loves creating all sorts of things with yarn. I've been crocheting for over 10 years, and it's my favorite way to unwind and get creative. I've ended up with a lot of leftover yarn in all kinds of colors and textures, perfect for small projects or trying out new techniques. Instead of letting it gather dust, I’m selling it at great prices so other crafters can enjoy it too. When I’m not crocheting, I'm usually sharing tips, hanging out at craft fairs, or connecting with fellow crafters. Thanks for stopping by, and I hope you find some yarn that sparks your next project! Happy crafting!
